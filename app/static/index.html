Proof‑of‑work

          
1) GET /api/pow?purpose=vote
2) find counter s.t. SHA256(token + ':' + counter) has N leading zero bits
3) send headers:
   X-POW-Token: <token>
   X-POW-Counter: <counter>

          
Difficulty is intentionally small for humans and moderate for spam resistance.
